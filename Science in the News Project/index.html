  • Focus on how AI generates sentences and paragraphs.

Analyze the Chatbot Summary

GenAI output is not always 100% accurate. As the editor, make sure the AI summary is accurate! Use lateral reading to check the references in the chatbot-generated summary to verify whether they exist.

For each reference link in the article:

  1. In a new tab, while keeping this split-screen tab open, open the link. Copy the title and search for it (Google or Google Scholar).
  2. Confirm the source:
    1. Does the link lead to a real, relevant page?
    2. What information does it provide?
  3. Evaluate Credibility:
    1. Who is the author? Do they have expertise?
    2. Any clear bias or conflicts of interest?
    3. Do other credible sources say the same thing?
  4. Check sourcing:
    1. Is the original primary source included?

A Powerful Tool

One of the most powerful uses of AI is as a tool to help you grow your skills. Instead of copying what it writes, you can use it to test your ideas, get feedback, and refine your thinking.

In the next step, you’ll use AI to improve your summary.

The goal is not for AI to rewrite your work, but to help you think more deeply about how to improve it.
