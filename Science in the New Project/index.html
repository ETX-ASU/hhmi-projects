Analyze the Chatbot Summary

Use lateral reading to check the references in the chatbot-generated summary article to verify whether they exist.

  1. Open each link. Copy the title and search for it in Google or Google Scholar.
  2. Confirm the source: Does the link lead to a real, relevant page? What information does it provide?
  3. Evaluate credibility: Who is the author? Do they have expertise? Any clear bias or conflicts of interest?
  4. Check sourcing: Is the original primary source included?

A Powerful Tool

One of the most powerful uses of AI is as a tool to help you grow your skills. Instead of copying what it writes, you can use it to test your ideas, get feedback, and refine your thinking.

In this activity, you’ll use AI to improve your own summary by making it clearer, more accurate, and more effective.

You stayed in control.

Throughout this activity, you guided AI, evaluated its accuracy, and used it to strengthen your own thinking and writing. AI can be a helpful tool, but it works best when you stay in control. Because it builds sentences based on patterns and probabilities, it can sound confident while still missing details or making small mistakes. That’s why it’s important to read AI-generated summaries critically, checking for accuracy, clarity, and completeness.

The real strength of AI isn’t just how powerful it is, but how you use it. When used well, AI can help sharpen your ideas, not replace them.
